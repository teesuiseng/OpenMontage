Example Paper Title: A Short Demonstration of Caption-Led Paper Videos

Abstract
This example paper text demonstrates how a committed text file can be converted into a caption-led video without committing binary media to git. We show that a GitHub Actions workflow can render frames, encode an MP4, and upload the result as a downloadable artifact.

Results
The generated video is intentionally simple, high contrast, and caption based. This keeps the pipeline reproducible in constrained environments where browser-based renderers or TTS providers may be unavailable.

Conclusion
This workflow is useful for previewing paper summaries, but the extracted claims are heuristic and should be checked against the original paper before public use.
